Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

I love chocolate chip cookies. These cookies should turn out soft and not too sweet.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder

Instructions

  1. Throw the dry ingredients into a bowl and mix for one min
  2. Throw in the wet ingredients and mix for another 2 mins
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees celsius
  4. Put dough into little balls and put in the oven for 15 mins
  5. Play it cool for 15 mins before eating
