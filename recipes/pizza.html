Pizza

Description

This is one of my favourite pizza recipes. I usually make this in the evening for my family. Feel free to change up or add any of your own ingredients. This is just a basic skeleton.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Pull out the ingredients
  2. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees celsius
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the pizza
  4. Put on the cheese and pepperoni
  5. Put in the oven for 20 mins
