Pasta

Description

This is a very easy recipe for those with little time or are feeling lazy. Feel free to add your own ingredients or change the sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce

Instructions

  1. Fill pot with water
  2. Boil water on the stove
  3. Put pasta noodles in water for 5 mins
  4. Rinse out the water
  5. Put in the sauce and mix
